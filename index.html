X and Y position simply adjusts the position of the lower left corner of the triangle.

Side Length adjusts length of all sides

Recursion Depth adjusts the amount of subtriangles contained within the master triangle

Colour is a comma delimited field to adjust RGB values of master triangle. Colour fade adjusts amount the RGB values change by each recursive call.

Line weight adjusts thickness of the drawn lines.

Animation Time adjusts the time in milliseconds for the "In and Out" animation complete. Master Time adjusts time in milliseconds for one full rotation.
